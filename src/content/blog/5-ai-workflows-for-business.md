Workflow automation has existed for years, but the introduction of AI has fundamentally changed what's possible. We are no longer limited to simple "If This, Then That" logic. Modern AI automation systems can now read, understand, categorize, and generate content dynamically.

Here are 5 AI-powered workflows we build for our clients to supercharge their business operations.

## 1. The Autonomous CRM Updater

**The Problem:** Sales reps hate logging notes in the CRM, leading to inaccurate data and missed opportunities.
**The AI Workflow:** 
Connect your call recording software (like Gong or Zoom) to a centralized AI platform. After a meeting, the AI transcribes the call, extracts key action items, sentiment, and BANT (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline) criteria, and automatically updates the respective HubSpot or Salesforce record.

## 2. Intelligent Customer Support Triage

**The Problem:** Support teams are overwhelmed by basic tickets, delaying responses to complex, high-value issues.
**The AI Workflow:**
Route all incoming Zendesk or Intercom tickets through a Large Language Model (LLM). The AI reads the ticket, categorizes the issue, determines the urgency, and checks internal documentation to see if it can generate a draft response. If it's a known issue, it drafts the reply for human approval. If it's complex, it routes it to the specialized tier-2 team immediately.

## 3. The Automated Content Marketing Engine

**The Problem:** Consistently producing high-quality content across multiple channels takes too much time.
**The AI Workflow:**
When a new long-form blog post is published (trigger), an automation tool sends the content to an AI agent. The agent is instructed to create:
- 3 LinkedIn posts
- A 5-tweet thread
- A short summary for the company newsletter
These drafts are then automatically dropped into a Slack channel or a Notion board for the marketing manager to review and schedule.

## 4. Invoice Processing and Data Extraction

**The Problem:** Manually entering invoice data into accounting software is prone to errors and incredibly tedious.
**The AI Workflow:**
When a vendor emails an invoice PDF, an automation platform intercepts the email and sends the PDF to an AI vision model. The AI extracts the vendor name, date, line items, total amount, and tax. It then formats this data and pushes it directly into QuickBooks or Xero as a draft bill, pinging the finance team in Slack for final approval.

## 5. Proactive Lead Enrichment and Scoring

**The Problem:** Marketing captures leads with minimal info (just an email address), making it hard for sales to prioritize.
**The AI Workflow:**
When a new email is captured via a Typeform or Webflow form, an automation triggers a script to scrape the lead's company website and LinkedIn presence. It feeds this unstructured data to an AI, asking it to summarize the company's value proposition and estimate their likelihood to buy your specific product. This "AI Score" and summary are appended to the CRM record before the sales rep even makes the first call.
